# CalmYoga: Future Feature Ideas and HarmonyOS Kit Integrations

This document outlines potential future features and enhancements for the CalmYoga app, leveraging various HarmonyOS kits to provide a more comprehensive and integrated experience on wearable devices.

## 1. Health Service Kit Integration
- **Heart Rate Monitoring**: Track the user's heart rate in real-time during yoga sessions to help them maintain a calm state or monitor physical exertion.
- **Calorie Tracking**: Calculate and record estimated calories burned based on the duration and type of yoga session, integrating this data into the user's overall health profile.

## 2. Notification Kit Integration
- **Daily Reminders**: Set up personalized daily or weekly notifications to remind users to take a moment for their yoga practice.
- **Achievement Alerts**: Notify users when they reach certain milestones, such as completing a 7-day streak or a specific number of total minutes.

## 3. Calendar Kit Integration
- **Session Scheduling**: Allow users to schedule their yoga sessions directly from the app and sync them with the HarmonyOS system calendar.
- **Practice History**: View a history of past sessions and upcoming planned sessions within the calendar interface.

## 4. Live View Kit Integration
- **Real-Time Progress**: Use the Live View Kit to show the current pose and remaining session time on the watch's main interface or Always-On Display (AOD), allowing users to track progress with a quick glance.
- **Active Session Feedback**: Provide brief, non-intrusive updates via Live View during transitions between poses.

## 5. Weather Service Kit Integration
- **Outdoor Session Recommendations**: Suggest outdoor-specific yoga sessions when the local weather is favorable.
- **Dynamic Content**: Adjust session recommendations based on weather conditions (e.g., focusing on warming poses during cold days or calming poses during hot weather).

## 6. PDF Kit Integration
- **Progress Reports**: Generate monthly PDF summaries of the user's yoga practice, including sessions completed, total time spent, and health metrics, which can be shared or saved.

## 7. Account Kit Integration
- **Cloud Sync**: Use Account Kit to securely sync user preferences, session history, and custom routines across different HarmonyOS devices.
